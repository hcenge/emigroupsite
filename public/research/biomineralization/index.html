Bio-Inspired Mineralization Creates Programmable Scaffolds

Bioengineering

Our peptides self-assemble into chiral ribbons that template mineral deposition. By altering sequence length we tune the resulting pore size distribution, enabling controlled nutrient transport.

In vivo tests show a 25% improvement in bone regeneration compared to commercial grafts. Future work will translate the method to load-bearing implants via 3D printing.
