AI-Assisted Materials Design Accelerates Discovery

Computation

We trained a graph neural network on more than 200,000 inorganic compounds to learn composition-structure-property relationships. By coupling the model with a multi-objective optimizer we quickly propose stable, high-conductivity candidates.

Recent validations using automated synthesis confirmed three new compositions with 15% higher ionic conductivity than the current state of the art.

The team now focuses on integrating uncertainty quantification so that experimental colleagues can prioritize the most promising AI suggestions.
