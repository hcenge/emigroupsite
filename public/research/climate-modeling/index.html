Regional Climate Models Improve Flood Forecasting

Climate

By assimilating radar precipitation data into a convection-permitting model we reduced false alarms by 30%. Partnerships with local agencies help translate the forecasts into evacuation plans.

The next phase will deploy lightweight models on edge devices placed at river gauges, providing rapid updates when connectivity is limited.
