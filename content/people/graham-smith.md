Dr Graham Smith is a Principal Scientist at the National Physical Laboratory (NPL), where he leads the Electrochemistry Group. His work focuses on electrochemical technologies including water electrolysers, polymer electrolyte membrane fuel cells, and emerging industrial electrochemical processes.

You can see Graham's full biography [here](https://www.npl.co.uk/our-people/graham-smith).
